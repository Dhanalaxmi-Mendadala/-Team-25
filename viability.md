# Viability Study – AI Prescription Helper & Analyzer

---

## 3️⃣ Economic Feasibility (Cost Analysis)

**Definition:**
Economic feasibility evaluates whether the proposed system is financially practical to develop, deploy, and maintain.

### Feasibility Status: **Medium to High**

### Development Cost

* **Frontend & Backend Development:**
  Low cost due to use of open-source technologies such as React Native, FastAPI, and SQLite.
* **Dataset Cost:**
  Free and publicly available datasets from Kaggle and other open medical resources.
* **AI Integration Cost:**
  Pay-per-use pricing model for AI APIs (Gemini/OpenAI), eliminating high upfront costs.

### Operational & Maintenance Cost

* Cloud server hosting (low to moderate)
* AI API usage for prescription analysis
* Periodic updates of medicine databases

### Cost Optimization Strategies

* Offline-first design minimizes AI API calls
* AI is used only for prescription analysis, not continuous input
* Caching frequently used medicine data

**Conclusion:**
The system is economically feasible for academic, prototype, and small-scale clinical use, with scalability possible at controlled costs.

---

## 4️⃣ Legal & Ethical Feasibility

**Definition:**
Legal and ethical feasibility assesses compliance with medical regulations, ethical standards, and data privacy requirements.

### Feasibility Status: **Medium (with safeguards)**

### Legal Considerations

* AI acts only as a decision-support tool
* Final prescription authority remains with the doctor
* No autonomous diagnosis or treatment decisions
* Minimal or no storage of sensitive patient data

### Ethical Considerations

* Transparency in AI-generated suggestions
* Prevention of biased or unsafe recommendations
* Protection of patient privacy

### Safeguards

* Clear disclaimer stating that AI suggestions are advisory only
* Limited patient data collection
* Local encryption for stored data

**Conclusion:**
The system is legally and ethically viable when positioned as an assistive clinical tool rather than a replacement for medical professionals.

## 6️⃣ Market Viability (Future Potential)

**Definition:**
Market viability evaluates the demand, acceptance, and long-term potential of the system.

### Viability Status: **High**

### Target Market

* Small clinics and private practitioners
* Rural and semi-urban hospitals
* Medical interns and junior doctors
* Telemedicine platforms
* Medical education institutions

### Market Demand Factors

* High incidence of prescription errors
* Time pressure on healthcare professionals
* Need for clear, readable prescriptions
* Rapid adoption of digital healthcare solutions

### Growth Opportunities

* Subscription-based clinic models
* Integration with telemedicine and EHR systems
* Multilingual prescription support
* Prescription analytics and reporting

**Conclusion:**
The system demonstrates strong future potential due to increasing digitalization in healthcare and the need for affordable prescription assistance tools.

Competitive viability analyzes how effectively the system can compete with existing solutions.

**Overall Summary:**
The AI Prescription Helper & Analyzer is economically practical, ethically responsible with safeguards, highly viable in the market, and competitively strong against existing prescription systems.
